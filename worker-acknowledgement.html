TaskMint Worker Acknowledgement Agreement

Before using TaskMint to send quotes, accept tasks, or perform services, you must read and agree to this Worker Acknowledgement.
By selecting “I Have Read and Accept,” you acknowledge that you understand and agree to the expectations, fees, payout rules, tax responsibilities, safety guidelines, and platform policies listed below.

1. Independent Contractor Status

I understand that I am an independent contractor and not an employee, partner, agent, or representative of TaskMint.

I am solely responsible for my own work performance, tools, equipment, transportation, business decisions, and any licenses, permits, or certifications required for the services I provide.

2. Off-Platform Payments Are Prohibited

I agree that I will not:

  • Request or accept payment outside of TaskMint.
  • Encourage customers to cancel a TaskMint booking to avoid platform fees.
  • Exchange payment information for the purpose of completing work outside the platform.
  • Attempt to move current or future TaskMint customers off the platform.
  • Ask customers to pay through Cash App, Venmo, Zelle, PayPal, cash, check, or any other outside payment method.

Violations may result in immediate account suspension, permanent removal from TaskMint, loss of unpaid earnings when permitted by law, and additional legal action when appropriate.

3. Platform Service Fee

I understand that TaskMint charges a 15% platform fee on completed tasks.

The platform fee helps support payment processing, customer support, platform maintenance, safety and security systems, worker tools, customer tools, and operating costs.

I understand this fee is deducted automatically from eligible payouts.

4. Payout Information

I understand that:

  • I must complete payout setup before receiving payments.
  • Payments are processed through Stripe or another approved TaskMint payment provider.
  • Payout timing may vary depending on banking institutions and payment providers.
  • TaskMint may temporarily delay payouts while investigating disputes, fraud, safety concerns, policy violations, chargebacks, account issues, or legal obligations.
  • I must keep my payout information accurate and up to date.

5. Taxes

I understand that I am responsible for my own taxes.

I acknowledge that:

  • TaskMint does not withhold federal, state, or local taxes on my behalf unless required by law.
  • I am responsible for reporting my income.
  • I may receive tax documentation if legally required.
  • I should consult a qualified tax professional regarding my personal tax obligations.
  • I am responsible for maintaining my own records of income, expenses, mileage, tools, supplies, and business-related costs.

6. Safety

I understand that my personal safety is my responsibility.

I agree to:

  • Use good judgment before accepting tasks.
  • Leave any situation that appears dangerous or unsafe.
  • Contact emergency services if immediate danger exists.
  • Never enter a location where I feel threatened.
  • Avoid carrying weapons or engaging in illegal activity while using TaskMint.
  • Review task details before accepting work.
  • Communicate through TaskMint whenever possible.

I understand that customers and workers should conduct their own due diligence regarding safety.

7. Professional Conduct

I agree to:

  • Treat customers respectfully.
  • Perform work honestly and professionally.
  • Communicate through TaskMint whenever possible.
  • Arrive on time or provide notice if delayed.
  • Follow all applicable laws and regulations.
  • Provide accurate quotes and task information.
  • Complete accepted work to the best of my ability.

I will not:

  • Harass, threaten, intimidate, or abuse customers.
  • Discriminate against customers.
  • Submit fraudulent quotes or false information.
  • Damage customer property intentionally.
  • Engage in illegal activity.
  • Use inappropriate, offensive, or threatening language.

8. Insurance and Liability

I understand that I am responsible for my own actions and conduct.

I acknowledge that:

  • TaskMint is a technology platform that connects customers and workers.
  • TaskMint does not supervise or control how work is performed.
  • I am responsible for obtaining any insurance coverage I believe is necessary.
  • I may be personally responsible for damage, injury, losses, or legal claims resulting from my actions.
  • I should not accept work that I am not qualified, prepared, or legally permitted to perform.

9. Account Suspension or Removal

TaskMint may suspend or permanently remove my account for reasons including but not limited to:

  • Fraud.
  • Off-platform payment attempts.
  • Repeated cancellations.
  • Safety violations.
  • Harassment or abuse.
  • Illegal activity.
  • False documentation.
  • Serious policy violations.
  • Misuse of customer information.
  • Failure to comply with TaskMint policies.

10. Customer and Worker Information

I understand that I may receive access to customer information only as necessary to complete a task.

I agree not to:

  • Sell or distribute customer information.
  • Misuse customer contact information.
  • Use customer information for unauthorized purposes.
  • Contact customers outside TaskMint for unrelated reasons.
  • Share customer addresses, phone numbers, messages, or personal details with others.
