Worker Terms & Agreement

Effective Date: May 19, 2026
This agreement is written for TaskMint platform use. It should be reviewed by a licensed attorney before launch.

These Worker Terms & Agreement govern access to and use of the TaskMint platform by workers, independent contractors, taskers, providers, vendors, or any individual offering services through TaskMint.

1. Independent Contractor Status

Workers using TaskMint are independent contractors and are not employees, agents, partners, franchisees, or representatives of TaskMint. Nothing in this agreement creates an employment relationship, partnership, joint venture, agency relationship, or entitlement to employee benefits.

Workers are responsible for:

  • Choosing whether to accept or reject work
  • Controlling how services are performed
  • Providing their own tools, equipment, transportation, labor, and supplies unless otherwise agreed
  • Managing their own schedules
  • Paying all applicable federal, state, and local taxes
  • Maintaining required licenses, permits, insurance, and certifications

2. Eligibility

Workers must be at least 18 years old, legally authorized to work, provide truthful information, and comply with all federal, state, county, municipal, and local laws that apply to the services they offer.

3. Verification

TaskMint may request identity verification, government ID, work history, certifications, or other verification information. TaskMint may approve, deny, suspend, or deactivate worker access based on safety, fraud prevention, account accuracy, or compliance concerns.

4. Background Checks

TaskMint may offer background checks in the future but does not currently require them unless separately stated. TaskMint reserves the right to introduce background checks, driving record checks, or additional verification requirements later.

5. Worker Responsibilities

Workers agree to:

  • Perform work professionally, safely, and lawfully
  • Arrive on time for accepted jobs
  • Communicate honestly with customers
  • Complete jobs according to accepted task details
  • Respect customers, property, and privacy
  • Use the arrival code system honestly
  • Follow TaskMint safety, messaging, payment, and dispute procedures

6. Prohibited Conduct

Workers may not:

  • Harass, threaten, intimidate, or discriminate against users
  • Use hateful, abusive, or inappropriate language
  • Request off-platform payment for TaskMint jobs
  • Submit false arrival codes, false evidence, or fraudulent claims
  • Misrepresent identity, skills, experience, licenses, or qualifications
  • Damage property or perform unsafe work
  • Use TaskMint for illegal, harmful, or prohibited services

7. Quotes & Accepted Jobs

Workers may submit quotes for available tasks. Customers are not required to accept any quote. Once a quote is accepted, the worker is expected to complete the task as agreed unless the task is canceled, rescheduled, disputed, or otherwise modified through TaskMint.

8. Payments, Fees & Payouts

TaskMint may collect customer payments, hold funds, deduct platform service fees, process refunds, manage dispute holds, and release payouts through third-party payment processors.

Workers authorize TaskMint and its payment providers to process payouts through ACH bank transfer, eligible debit card, or other approved payout method.

Payouts may be delayed due to fraud prevention, payment processing, account verification, dispute review, banking delays, chargebacks, legal requirements, or platform security reviews.

9. Taxes

Workers are responsible for all tax obligations, including federal, state, and local income taxes, self-employment taxes, business taxes, licensing fees, and reporting obligations. TaskMint may issue tax forms when legally required.

10. Disputes

TaskMint may review disputes involving payment, job completion, cancellation, safety, property damage, misconduct, fraud, arrival verification, or customer complaints.

TaskMint may review messages, photos, timestamps, task details, arrival codes, payment records, uploaded evidence, and account history when investigating disputes.

TaskMint may temporarily hold funds during a dispute and may make platform decisions regarding payouts, refunds, account restrictions, or deactivation.

11. Safety

Workers are responsible for evaluating job conditions and leaving unsafe environments. Workers should contact emergency services if there is immediate danger. TaskMint does not supervise physical job sites and cannot guarantee customer behavior or job-site safety.

12. Insurance

Workers are responsible for maintaining any legally required insurance, including auto insurance, liability insurance, commercial coverage, professional coverage, or workers’ compensation coverage where required by law.

13. Reviews & Ratings

Customers may leave ratings and reviews after completed jobs. Workers may be allowed to respond publicly one time. Reviews, ratings, and responses must not include threats, harassment, hate speech, private personal information, profanity, or false claims.

14. Communications

TaskMint may monitor, store, moderate, and review platform communications for safety, fraud prevention, support, legal compliance, and dispute resolution. Users must not share prohibited payment details, threats, harassment, or illegal content through the messaging system.

15. Account Suspension & Termination

TaskMint may suspend, restrict, or terminate worker access for violations of this agreement, fraud concerns, safety risks, repeated disputes, false information, unlawful activity, abusive behavior, payment issues, or platform misuse.

16. Privacy & Data Use

TaskMint may collect and process worker information for account management, verification, payments, tax reporting, fraud prevention, support, analytics, legal compliance, and platform operations.

17. Intellectual Property

TaskMint owns its platform, brand, design, software, logos, content, workflows, and technology. Workers may not copy, reverse engineer, scrape, misuse, or commercially exploit TaskMint systems or branding.

18. Limitation of Liability

To the fullest extent permitted by law, TaskMint is not liable for indirect damages, lost profits, lost opportunities, customer conduct, worker conduct, job-site injuries, property damage, payment delays, platform interruptions, third-party service failures, or events outside TaskMint’s reasonable control.

19. Indemnification

Workers agree to defend, indemnify, and hold harmless TaskMint from claims, damages, losses, liabilities, costs, and expenses arising from worker conduct, negligence, legal violations, tax obligations, insurance failures, contract breaches, injuries, property damage, or misuse of the platform.

20. Arbitration & Class Action Waiver

To the fullest extent permitted by law, disputes may be resolved through binding individual arbitration. Workers waive participation in class, collective, representative, or consolidated actions unless prohibited by applicable law.

21. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by applicable federal law and the laws of the State of Georgia unless another jurisdiction’s law is required. Workers remain responsible for complying with all federal, state, county, city, and local laws applicable to the work performed.

22. Changes to This Agreement

TaskMint may update this agreement at any time. Continued use of TaskMint after changes are posted means the worker accepts the updated terms.

23. Entire Agreement

This agreement represents the full agreement between TaskMint and the Worker regarding worker use of the platform. If any section is found unenforceable, the remaining sections remain in effect.
